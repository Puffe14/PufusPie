Explanation

One plays as the meteor and the others as its next victims.
During meteor's turn 'click' squares to vanquish players or block their paths.
When you have zero attacks to make anymore, press 'enter' to advance to player turn.
During players's turns, use 'arrow keys' to pass trough empty squares to hide from the meteor.
Press 'enter' to advance to the next players turn until meteor's turn starts again.
Players leave trails on the square that were hiding on unless if they are standing still.
Players must hide and move unpredictably to attempt not to perish for as long as possible.
